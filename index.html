My Hungary

Most of you probably don't know where Hungary is. It's because Hungary is really small. I've met some people who were not kids and they never even heard of Hungary. If you want to know where Hungary is, how it looks like and what the culture is then just keep reading my article

Hungary is in Central Europe. We have seven neighbouring countries: Austria, Slovakia, Ukraine, Romania, Slovenia, Croatia, Serbia. Seven Hungaries could fit in to Texas because Hungary is only slightly smaller than Indiana. Hungary is landlocked country which means that we don't have any seaside.

It is in the Central European timezone so we are 7 ours ahead of Texas. Right now the time here is 11:10 AM and in Hungary it is 6:10 PM. People in Hungary speak Hungarian. We are the only country that speaks this language. It is unlike any European languages. There are 54 letters in our alphabet.
